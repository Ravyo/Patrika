Freshers party 2k17

Freshers Party is the most awaited event at the inception of the semester at any College. The innovative and promising tradition at Aravali College of Engineering and Management adds new glorious dimensions to such events. The College organized a Freshers Party on 1st Sept, 2017 in the campus. The event was inaugurated by Sh. Dhan Singh Bhadana (Chairman), Sh. Mehtab Singh Bhadana (Vice Chairman), Dr. Suresh Kumar Jindal(Director) and Sh. Sanjay Singh Bhanot (Dean). It was an ecstasy filled evening, at which the fresher got an opportunity not only to showcase their talents but also got an opportunity to interact with the seniors.
